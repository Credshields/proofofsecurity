Proof of Security is a brand by SolidityScan that includes various activities like CTFs, Hackathons, Workshops, Meet-ups, Summit, etc.

PROOF OF SECURITY SUMMIT ‘23 - INDIA

Hosted by
In Associated Partner
5th DECEMBER2023

BENGALURU, INDIA

The Proof of Security Summit aims to bring together leading experts, developers, researchers, and enthusiasts in the blockchain and Web3Security space. This event will focus on advancing security practices in Fostering collaboration and sharing best practices to ensure the integrity and fortification of blockchain applications.
